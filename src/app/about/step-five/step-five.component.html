2004/5
During the period in north India he was often invited to the bussiness and films city of Mumbai. After constantly traveling back and forth between north and south India he settled in Mumbai. Shortly after he was offered to become the spiritual counselor & reader at the Grand Hayyat Celina which is the largest Hotel in Asia. During this period Ofer became known between the city Celebs and the bussiness community. It was the time he started to make his national media appearances, his success attracted people from all over Asia. His workshop and spiritual events at the Hayyat Spa attracted people who seek his forecast and quidance. It was the time when his name started to be recognize by far community's in USA, Canada and middle east.

2005/6
Around late 2003 while living between the south beaches of India and the northern mountains region he went through a deep spiritual transformation a period he describe as an enlightenment years. He then started to write his first book "the light" the book was partly written in Automatic writing (sub conciseness writing), he was in a unique state of mind when writing this spiritual manuscript. The book contains an enlightening description of the human soul journey. The book took many years to complete and waited years to be translated to English. "THE LIGHT" was completed during the time of his spiritual work in London and finally brought to publish in 2012.
