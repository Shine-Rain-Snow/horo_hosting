THE WORLD
The World card represents a union with the whole, realization of your place in the Universe, a strong feeling of oneness with everything. Your problem fade with the sense that you are in touch with the entire Universe. This is a very good card for psychic power, for insight into hidden things, and for completing any sort of material or spiritual task.
